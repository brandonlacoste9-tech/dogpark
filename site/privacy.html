Privacy policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026 · Politique de confidentialité · Política de privacidad

01 — Introduction

This static dog park directory helps you find parks mapped in OpenStreetMap across Canada, the United States, and Mexico. This policy explains what limited data we handle and how advertising works on the site.

02 — Information we collect

  • Local browser storage — language, theme, and visitor reviews you choose to write (reviews never leave your browser on this static site).
  • Technical data — standard web server / CDN logs (IP, user agent, pages) if provided by the host (e.g. Vercel).
  • Cookies / identifiers — from advertising partners when ads are shown (see below).

03 — How we use information

We use this information to run and improve the directory, remember your preferences, keep optional local reviews on your device, and display advertising via Google AdSense.

04 — Advertising & third-party cookies (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to show ads. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar identifiers to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other sites, measure ad performance, and personalize advertising.

  • Ad provider: Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC (AdSense)
  • Publisher ID: ca-pub-4276130467303652
  • Manage personalized ads: adssettings.google.com
  • Google ads technologies: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • You can also block some cookies in your browser settings or with an ad blocker.

05 — OpenStreetMap data

Park listings come from OpenStreetMap and are available under the ODbL. Tags can be incomplete — always confirm hours and rules on site.

06 — Contact

Questions about privacy or ads on this site: use your hosting / project contact channel, or open an issue on the project repository if public.
